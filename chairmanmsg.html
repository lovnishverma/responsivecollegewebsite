Chairman's Message

Education plays a vital role in the modernization and development of a nation. It serves as the foundation for comprehensive growth—both material and spiritual—by refining our sensitivities and perceptions. Education fosters national cohesion, nurtures a scientific mindset, and promotes a spirit of interdependence, further advancing the principles of socialism, secularism, and democracy enshrined in our constitution. Additionally, it plays a crucial role in unlocking human potential across various economic sectors. No educational program can succeed without well-trained teachers. A robust professional education program for teachers is essential for improving the quality of education. To achieve this noble objective, colleges of education were established with the specific purpose of training and equipping educators with the necessary skills and competencies. Through a systematic approach, teacher education programs can be revitalized and modernized to enhance their effectiveness. Various innovations have been introduced to empower teachers with the expertise required for effective teaching. Since its inception, our college has been dedicated to this mission, making a significant contribution to the field of teacher education.

Sh. P.K. Johar
Chairman
